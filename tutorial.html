Motivation

The children have not come back from playing in the woods. Find them and destroy anything that gets in your way.

Movement

WASD and IJKL keys.

Spell Casting

Spells can be found on scrolls. They'll look like

srv-nio

To invoke that fake spell press "s" "r" "v" in any order and release simultaneously, then do the same thing for "n" "i" and "o".

Backspace undoes the most recent spell segment.

Spell Book

The only way to see which spells you have learned is by casting the spell

rbil
