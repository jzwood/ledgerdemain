Motivation

Löta and Svlöti have not returned from playing in the forest. Find them — baby witches should not be left unattended for long.

Spell Casting

Spells can be found on scrolls. They'll look like

srv-nio

To invoke the illustratory spell above press "s" "r" "v" in any order then release simultaneously. Repeat for "n" "i" and "o".

Backspace undoes the most recent spell segment.
