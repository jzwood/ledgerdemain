Legerdemain

Motivation

Löta and Svlöti have not returned from playing in the forest. Find them — baby witches should not be left unattended for long.

Spell Casting *

Spells can be found on scrolls. An example spell:

ech-snui

is comprised of two key clusters: "ech" and "snui". For each cluster (left-to-right), press then release all keys simultaneously.

Successfully registered clusters will appear in the top left corner of the screen. Backspace deletes the most recent spell segment.

The latest invoked spell will be visible in the top right corner of the screen.

Spell Book

Your spell book records spells discovered in the forest. To both consult it and stow it away cast:

rbil

Movement *
